Pad Rater


Price

  1) What is your monthly housing budget?
  2) What is the monthly rent per person?
  3) What will each person be paying in utilities?
  4) How much will each person be spending monthly on insurance?

Location

  1) How many miles will you have to commute to work or class?
  2) How will you make this commute?
  3) Are any of these within walking distance of the apartment? (Hold shift to select multiple)
  4) How about these? (Hold shift to select multiple)

Size

  1) How many people will be living in the apartment?
  2) How many bedrooms does the apartment have?
  3) How many bathrooms does the apartment have?
  4) How many square feet are in the apartment (If you don't know, make your best guess)?

Landlord

  1) Was it easy to contact the landlord?
  2) Is the landlord willing to negotiate terms of lease?
  3) Will the landlord put commitments to you in writing?

Lease Terms

  1) Will you have to pay any of the followings? (Hold shift to select multiple)
  2) Are utilities included?
  3) Will you be staying the full term of the lease?
  4) Will the term of the lease be extended automatically?
  5) Does the rent have late fees?

Misc

  1) Are the following in suitable working condition? (Hold shift to select multiple)
  2) Will the landlord walk through the property with you to note any repairs the apartment needs before you move in?
